CDIPS Amazon Product Reviews

Our work in the 3 week CDIPS workshop is displayed in this dashboard.
Provided with a corpus of product reviews from amazon we set out to do 3 things, sentiment classification, product review trends, and topic modeling.
Visit our other pages to see the results.
